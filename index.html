ABOUT COSNICS

Cosnics offers a flexible training and collaboration platform. It is a free and open source Learning CMS.
The goal of Cosnics is to quickly and easily (co-)create and deliver reusable learning content.

All users in Cosnics have their own personal drive: content can be created, published or shared from this repository.
In Cosnics users can easily connect to each other in workspaces. Cosnics can also connect to external repositories like Google Drive & Youtube.

Manage your training in a fast and flexible way with Cosnics! The main applications are:
  • Personal drive (repository)
  • Personal homepage
  • Workspaces
  • Courses (LMS)
Cosnics is also an easy and effective communication tool : it allows you to mimic your organization structure in the platform groups.
Cosnics is the complete makeover of the Chamilo LCMS Connect project.
